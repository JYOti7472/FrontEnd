Vaccines for children

Vaccines will help protect your child against diseases that can cause serious harm or death, especially in people with developing immune systems like infants. It's important to vaccinate your child.Without vaccines, your child is at risk of becoming seriously ill or even dying from childhood diseases such as measles and whooping cough.
